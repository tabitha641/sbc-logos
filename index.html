  3. Find the server named Home — then watch your way:

Get SBC Alerts

A free ping when a special-day channel goes live or a new show hits the dial. No account, no cost, unsubscribe anytime.

  1. Get the free ntfy app (iPhone or Android), or just open ntfy.sh/sbc-now in any browser.
  2. Subscribe to the topic sbc-now — scan the code, or type it in.
  3. Done. SBC news comes straight to your phone.
